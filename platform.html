Real-Time Computer Vision

Our DETR-based detection engine processes video streams at 30 FPS, identifying persons, vehicles, animals, and objects with industry-leading accuracy. Multi-object tracking maintains identity across frames.

  • 99.7% detection accuracy across 80+ object classes
  • Sub-50ms latency from capture to detection
  • Unlimited simultaneous person tracking

Privacy-Compliant Face Recognition

Biometric verification for enrolled personnel only. Our system never stores face data for unknown individuals, ensuring full compliance with GDPR, BIPA, and the EU AI Act.

  • Multi-angle recognition with 46+ sample enrollment
  • Cross-camera identity persistence without re-scanning
  • Zero biometric storage for unknown persons

Vision Language Model Analysis

Advanced AI understands scene context and generates natural language descriptions. Mode-specific prompts optimize threat detection for home, away, night, and guest scenarios.
